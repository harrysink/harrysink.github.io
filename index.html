About EE

Quality, Standard and Control

Eagle Endeavour LTD is an Oil & Gas company founded with the aim of providing Engineering, Procurement, Installation, Construction & Start-up Assistance, Inspection, and Consultancy services to our clients, in order to meet the ever-changing challenges of the Oil & Gas sector, other sectors of the economy, and the managerial obligations therein.

We have a team of highly educated professionals with wide technical exposure in the industry who are experienced enough to find solutions to the ever rising needs of engineering procurement, construction, installation, maintenance works and human needs in general.
