PROJECTS


Take a look at projects I have worked on, both personal and contracted



Eagle Endeavour Ltd.

Eagle Endeavour Ltd. is an Oil & Gas company founded with the aim of providing Engineering, Procurement, Installation, Construction & Start-up Assistance, Inspection, and Consultancy services to their clients, in order to meet the ever-changing challenges of the Oil & Gas sector, other sectors of the economy, and the managerial obligations therein.


Steakholder Inc.

Steakholder is a fictional steakhouse business that caters to individuals and fellow businesses by providing and supplying premium grilled steak for small and large scale consumption. Steakhouse owns and operates three branches within its founding city. Customers can visit their physical restaurants, or place an order online to get their steak delivered to them. Steakhouse prides itself on its unique stone grill method of preparing steak, which most steakhouses don't employ, giving it a sort of monotony in the steakhouse business.


Movie Box

Movie Box is movie reference website that dynamically fetches and displays updated movie information from TheMovieDataBase (TMDB), by means of an API.


Emmanuella's Portfolio

This is a personal portfolio site for Ui/Ux designer, Emmanuella Kehinde, built with HTML & CSS for structure and styling, and Animate-On-Scroll (AOS) for scroll effects.


Noughts&Crosses

Remember our favorite childhood paper game for when there isn't a teacher in class? Noughts&Crosses is my implementation of all that scribbling from unforgtable memory, x & o. It is a React webapp bootsraped with Vite, and written in Typescript. It promises a very responsive experience with catchy sound feedbacks while playing and starting a new game. For the best playing experience, view on desktop.
